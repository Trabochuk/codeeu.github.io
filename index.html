Why #codeEU?

Coding is fun. Creative. Full of great people and passionate communities.

You're already passionate about helping others see the beauty in code. The fun in building stuff.

But you can't yet convince your friends to start learning. Or maybe you are willing to organize a workshop, but are not so keen about talking with sponsors and doing all the PR. Yeah, we know the feeling.

Stronger together!

So here's the deal: we want to connect individuals, groups, organizations, and companies, who are able to help more people experience the joy of coding.

With your help, we can start a big fire that will be seen outside our usual circles. A fire so big it can no longer be ignored by the big media, no matter what the latest celebrity gossip is. We know how much fun it is to build things and we're ready to share our passion.

Sounds interesting? Well then, let's all do something really big between:

November 25 - 30, 2013

Organize a programming workshop, the one you planned to do in any case. Find a few friends who're curious about what you do and teach them how to code. Start a local study or support group for coding beginners. And remember, two is a group already.

Are you in? Join the newsletter and we'll let you know all the details very soon.

P.S.: We're also looking for sponsors of any size to support local events. More info soon.
